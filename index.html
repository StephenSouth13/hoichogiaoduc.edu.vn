Education & Study Abroad Fair

HCM City 2025

The Education & Study Abroad Fair in HCM City 2025 is a large-scale event connecting students, parents with universities, educational organizations, and reputable study abroad consulting units.

  • OBJECTIVE

    Connecting students with universities and educational organizations both domestically and internationally, providing information on study abroad, scholarships, and international career orientation.

  • MEANING

    This event serves as a bridge helping students access information on study abroad, scholarships, and global career opportunities. It creates a space for connecting between universities, educational organizations, and businesses, opening new pathways for the youth.
